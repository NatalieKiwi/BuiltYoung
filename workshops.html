What We Teach

WORKSHOP TOPICS

Every session is built around a decision young founders actually face, taught by people who have made that decision at scale.

Workshop 01
HOW TO INTERVIEW C LEVEL TALENT WHEN YOU ARE STILL EARLY

AI can polish a resume. It cannot fully fake judgment, leadership, and operating depth. Learn how to ask better questions, evaluate senior candidates, and communicate with C level talent as a young founder.

Workshop 02
HOW A 20 PERSON TEAM STARTS OPERATING LIKE 50

The team does not break because you grow. It breaks because no one redesigned how decisions, ownership, and communication work. This session covers the structural shifts that make small teams punch above their weight.

Workshop 03
FINDING A COFOUNDER WHO CAN BUILD WITH YOU, NOT JUST DREAM WITH YOU

A good cofounder is not just someone you like. It is someone whose judgment, risk tolerance, work style, and ambition can survive pressure with yours. We cover how to evaluate, test, and formalize the right partnership.

Workshop 04
YOUR FIRST LEADERSHIP HIRES: WHO TO HIRE, WHEN, AND WHY

Before you hire a VP, know whether you need a strategist, operator, builder, closer, or player coach. This session gives you the framework to make the right leadership hire at the right stage.

Workshop 05
BUILDING A TEAM THAT MATCHES THE COMPANY YOU ARE BECOMING

Early hiring is not about filling seats. It is about designing the next version of the company. This session covers how to hire for where you are going, not just where you are, and how to build a team culture that scales without you micromanaging every piece of it.
