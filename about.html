Why Built Young Exists

WE FIND THE SIGNAL BEFORE IT BECOMES OBVIOUS

I spent years evaluating C level talent for publicly listed companies. That work taught me one thing: the best people rarely look the way you expect. The signal is in how they think, not how they present.

Built Young applies that same lens to young founders. We find builders under 25 with real revenue, real customers, and real pressure, and we tell their stories before anyone else does.

AGE IS NOT THE SIGNAL. EXECUTION IS.

We do not spotlight founders simply because they are young. We spotlight them because they are already building, selling, learning, and making hard decisions.

REAL REVENUE MATTERS.

Ideas are easy to admire. Revenue is harder to fake. We care about customers, traction, and the messy work behind building something people actually want.

EARLY DOES NOT MEAN UNPROVEN.

Some founders are early in visibility, but not early in effort. Built Young recognizes the people doing serious work before the outside world knows their name.

BUILDING SOMETHING REAL, OR KNOW SOMEONE WHO IS?

If you are under 30 and building from scratch, or if you know a founder who deserves to be seen, we want to hear from you.
