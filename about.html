Meet the Host

NATALIE CHU

Host & Founder · Built Young Podcast

Built Young is hosted by Natalie Chu, a headhunter turned founder advocate with deep roots in B2B sales, org design, and talent strategy. She built a bridge between young operators doing real revenue and the investors who want to back them before anyone else does.

Having personally interviewed hundreds of C level executives at listed companies, Natalie brings a rare perspective on what success actually looks like from the inside: the mindset, the decisions, and the moments that defined it. Profoundly inspired by the stories of young builders who refused to wait, she created Built Young to pay that forward.

Think of her as the smart older sister who has seen it from both sides and genuinely wants you to win.
