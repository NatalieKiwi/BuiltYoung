About Built Young

I STARTED BUILT YOUNG BECAUSE YOUNG FOUNDERS REMINDED ME WHAT ACTION LOOKS LIKE.

After years of working with C-level talent, I started Built Young to spotlight young founders building real companies before the world knows their names, and to share the mindset that helps more people take action.

NATALIE CHU

LinkedIn →

After speaking with more than 500 senior to C-level leaders as an executive recruiter for many years, I learned that the strongest people are not defined by titles alone. Their real edge is in how they think, make decisions, handle pressure, and lead through uncertainty.

Built Young brings that perspective to young founders, spotlighting not only what they are building, but the mindset and traits that help them become the next generation of entrepreneurs.
